Shipping Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2025

At Global Shopping, we strive to provide reliable and efficient shipping services to our customers worldwide. This Shipping Policy outlines our shipping procedures, delivery times, and related information.

1. Shipping Methods

We offer several shipping methods to accommodate different needs:

  • Standard Shipping: 7-15 business days
  • Express Shipping: 3-7 business days
  • Premium Shipping: 1-3 business days

Shipping methods may vary depending on your location and the items you purchase.

2. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the following factors:

  • Destination country and region
  • Selected shipping method
  • Weight and dimensions of the package
  • Total order value

The exact shipping cost will be displayed during checkout before you complete your purchase.

3. Free Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50 to select countries. This offer may not be available for all locations and may be subject to change during promotional periods.

4. Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. During peak seasons or promotional periods, processing time may be extended.

5. Shipping Confirmation

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your package using the provided tracking number on our website or the carrier's website.

6. Delivery Time

Estimated delivery times are as follows:

  • Domestic (China): 1-5 business days
  • Asia Pacific: 3-10 business days
  • North America: 7-15 business days
  • Europe: 7-15 business days
  • Rest of the World: 10-20 business days

Please note that these are estimated delivery times and may vary due to factors beyond our control, such as customs clearance, weather conditions, or local delivery circumstances.

7. International Shipping

For international orders, please be aware of the following:

  • Import duties, taxes, and customs fees are not included in the product price or shipping cost.
  • These charges are the buyer's responsibility and may vary depending on your country's customs policies.
  • Customs clearance may cause delays in delivery.
  • We are not responsible for any customs-related delays or charges.

8. Address Accuracy

Please ensure that your shipping address is complete and accurate. We are not responsible for delivery issues arising from incorrect or incomplete address information. Any packages returned due to incorrect address information may incur additional shipping charges for redelivery.

9. Shipping Restrictions

Some products may not be available for shipping to certain countries due to import restrictions or regulations. If a product cannot be shipped to your location, you will be notified during the checkout process.

10. Lost or Damaged Packages

In the rare event that your package is lost or damaged during transit:

  • Please contact our customer service team within 14 days of the expected delivery date.
  • We will work with the shipping carrier to locate your package or process a claim.
  • Depending on the circumstances, we may offer a replacement, refund, or store credit.

11. Changes to Shipping Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Shipping Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website.
